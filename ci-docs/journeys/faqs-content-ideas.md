# Responsible AI FAQs for content ideas

These responsible AI FAQs explain how the content ideas feature in Dynamics 365 Customer Insights - Journeys works, what it can do, and its limitations.

## What is the content ideas feature?

Content ideas is a feature in the Customer Insights - Journeys email designer that helps you start an email draft and explore different ways to express your message. To generate ideas, select **Content ideas** in the email editor and add two to five key points that you want to cover. Each key point can be a few words or a full sentence. The more specific your key points are, the more precise the generated output is. Content ideas generates paragraphs of text based on your input that you can use in marketing emails. Browse the generated ideas in the content ideas pane, and then select **Add to my draft** to copy the content into your email.

## What can the content ideas feature do?

Content ideas is built on a machine learning model called Generative Pre-trained Transformer (GPT)-3. Trained on a large set of text samples from the internet, GPT-3 generates new text in English that can sound like it was written by a person. Content ideas uses GPT-3 as a foundation and, if enabled, considers your organization's recent ready-to-send emails and the key messaging points that you share for each new email draft. Content ideas masks personal data in generated ideas and shows only results that are long enough and unique enough to be useful.

If you grant permission, content ideas automatically reviews your organization's recent English ready-to-send emails without human review. The only time a person reviews generated content is if you report it to Microsoft by using the quick feedback survey built into the content ideas feature.

## What is the intended use of content ideas?

The content ideas feature is intended to simplify email creation. Marketers can use content ideas to turn themes or ideas into full paragraphs for campaigns. Text generated by content ideas isn't meant to be used without manual review or supervision.

## How was content ideas evaluated? What metrics are used to measure performance?

Content ideas underwent substantial testing before the feature was released. Content ideas relies on user feedback to report inappropriate content. If you encounter inappropriate content being generated, report it to Microsoft using this feedback form: [Report abuse](https://msrc.microsoft.com/report/abuse?ThreatType=URL&IncidentType=Responsible%20AI&SourceUrl=https://dynamics.microsoft.com/marketing/overview/). Your feedback helps improve the functionality moving forward.

Microsoft may disable the content ideas feature for selected customers if abuse of the functionality is detected.

## What are the limitations of content ideas? How can users minimize their impact?

Content ideas generates original content, but it isn't always factual. Because the feature relies on AI trained on a wide range of internet sources, some text suggestions might be questionable or inappropriate. Review and edit generated suggestions so your final copy is accurate and appropriate.

## What operational factors and settings support effective and responsible use of content ideas?

Follow these practices to get the most from the content ideas feature:

1. Provide relevant key points that describe your message: use up to five descriptive key points, write each one as a phrase or full sentence, and keep them focused on the same topic.
1. Use high-quality text in your 20 or more recent **Ready-to-send** commercial marketing emails. Those emails, together with the key points you provide, help the AI service generate personalized ideas.
1. Always review and edit content ideas suggestions before publishing them in an email or marketing campaign.
