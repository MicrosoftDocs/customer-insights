# Open the Power Apps customization window

Most types of customizations require you to work in the **Power Apps** customization window. To get there:

1. Open the **Settings** menu (:::image type="icon" source="media/settings-icon.png" border="false":::) at the top of the page, and then select **Advanced settings**. The advanced-settings-area opens in a new browser tab. This area uses a horizontal navigator at the top of the page instead of a side navigator.

1. Go to **Settings** > **Customization** > **Customizations**, and then select **Customize the System** on the **Customization** page.

1. A **Power Apps** customization window opens. Use the left pane to select the item you want to customize, and use the pane on the right to change its settings.

    :::image type="content" source="media/calendar-powerapps.png" alt-text="Screenshot of the Power Apps customization window showing the navigation tree on the left and settings pane on the right." lightbox="media/calendar-powerapps.png":::

For complete details about how to customize model-driven apps in Dynamics 365, see the [Power Apps documentation](/powerapps/?panel=getstarted#pivot=home).
